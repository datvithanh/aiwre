Welcome

Welcome to the Homepage of the Artificial Intelligence and Water Resources Engineering research group.

Our group was co-founded by eight researchers with a variety of profiles, skills and experiences, who form a dynamic and unique group of experts. Our team members with diverse academic backgrounds (civil engineering, water resources, environment, computer science, climatology, and social sciences) are trained in top- tier universities in U.S.A, Germany, the Netherlands, Australia, New Zealand, Japan, and Singapore. Ranging from young people leading international water networks while still pursuing their studies, to senior experts with decades of qualified experience working for different academic and consulting environments, we are a truly intergenerational and multidisciplinary team.

We would like to use this portal as a platform for global networking to engage and connect with potential collaborators and customers to provide you with in-depth information on our group missions. We hope you will find this website informative, useful and user-friendly. If you have any comments or suggestions for us, our work or services, please do let us know.
